DORI S. REVNESS, MSEd

Trainer / Facilitator

Dori is the inspirational force behind Progressive Performance Solutions, LLC. She is a PERFORMANCE CONSULTANT/CERTIFIED POSITIVE PSYCHOLOGY PRACTITIONER. She holds a certificate in Applied Positive Psychology (CAPP) from the Flourishing Center. The CAPP training provides expertise in the theory, science and application of positive psychology. A gifted trainer and facilitator she is able to consult with organizations to provide innovative, practical and sustainable learning solutions that help clients solve business problems. She specializes in strategy execution, curriculum/workshop design & development, and workshop facilitation. She offers unique programs/workshops in resilience and positive psychology skills. Her experience includes both mid-tier and large healthcare and pharmaceutical companies.
